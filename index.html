Japan

Location

Japan is an island country in East Asia, located in the northwest Pacific Ocean.

Language

The most widely spoken language in Japan is Japanese, which is separated into several dialects with Tokyo dialect considered standard Japanese. In addition to the Japanese language, Ryukyuan languages are spoken in Okinawa and parts of Kagoshima in the Ryukyu Islands.

Culture

Shinto and Buddhism are the primary religions of Japan. Home of Sumo wrestling, kimono, bonsai trees, samurai sword, mt. fuji, Temples and shrines.

Food

Japan national dish curry rice. Famous dishes are Gyoza, Sushi,Ramen,and Tempura.

Philippines

Location

The Philippines, officially the Republic of the Philippines, is an archipelagic island country in Southeast Asia. It is situated in the western Pacific Ocean, and consists of about 7,640 islands, that are broadly categorized under three main geographical divisions from north to south: Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao.

Language

Eight (8) major dialects spoken by majority of the Filipinos: Tagalog, Cebuano, Ilocano, Hiligaynon or Ilonggo, Bicolano, Waray, Pampango, and Pangasinense. Tagalog is the national language.

Culture

The Filipinos are family oriented and often religious with an appreciation for art, fashion, music and food. The national costume are the Barong and Barot Saya. Famous public transportations are jeepney and tricycle.

Food

Famous Filipino dishes are Adobo, Lechon, Pancit, Pinakbet, KareKare, Sinigang and Crispy Pata.
